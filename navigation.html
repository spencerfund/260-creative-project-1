PLAN YOUR TRIP

ACCESS BY CAR

The Atomium is accessible via the A201 and the E40
15 minutes from De Brouckère Metro Station
20 minutes from Brussels South Train Station
20-30 minutes from Brussels International Airport

ACCESS BY METRO

From De Brouckère Metro Station:
Take Metro 1 to Beekant Station,
switch and take Metro 6 to Heysel / Heizel Station

From Brussels South Train Station:
Take Metro 6 to Heysel / Heizel station

From Brussels Internation Airport:
Take Bus 12 (airport line) to Trône / Troon Station,
switch and take Metro 6 to Heysel / Heizel Station

NEARBY ATTRACTIONS

ADAM - Brussels Design Museum

The only museum in Brussels, dedicated to design from the XXth century until today, situated next to the Atomium.

Mini-Europe

Located at the foot of the Atomium, Mini-Europe is the only park where you can visit the whole of Europe in a couple of hours. A truly unique voyage! Stroll amid the typical ambiance of the most beautiful towns of the Old Continent.

The Brussels Planetarium

The Planetarium of the Royal Observatory of Belgium, one of the largest in Europe, is a magical place to discover the beauty of the starry sky. Enter the magic of the Planetarium and enjoy a world without boundaries.

Chinese Pavilion and Japanese Tower

The Chinese Pavilion, situated amidst the green surroundings of the royal estate at Laeken, together with the nearby Japanese Tower, form a remarkable exotic ensemble built at the beginning of the 20th century.
